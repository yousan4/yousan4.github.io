Moroccan pancakes

Moroccan Pancakes or Beghrir are a soft spongy and thick pancakes with many holes on top. made with yeast and semolina or durum wheat flour. They are served with honey butter or honey with roasted almond slivers.

Moroccan Gourmet (bissara)

Moroccan Bissara An adaptable recipe for Split Pea Bessara, a satisfying Moroccan pureed split pea soup or dip. Although this variation of bessara has lots of flavor on its own, it's extra delicious when garnished with olive oil, cumin, and cayenne.
